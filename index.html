Introduction

The next phase of web3 depends on verifiable automation. This litepaper outlines ProjectName’s architecture for trust-minimized onchain agents.

Problem

Despite progress in wallet access and DeFi tooling, automation remains opaque and risky. Existing bots lack verifiability, and AI agents introduce new vectors for errors and exploits.

Solution

ProjectName introduces scoped autonomy, verifiable integrity, and reputation-based agent ecosystems secured with ZKPs and TEEs.

Architecture

Our system consists of smart accounts (ERC-4337), zkPermissions, and an execution orchestrator with operators submitting attested and proven actions.

Summary

Verifiable automation offers a trust layer for agents, enabling secure delegation, transparent action, and scalable web3 interactions.
